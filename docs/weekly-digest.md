# Weekly Digest

Every week: ≤10 fresh reads from the 16 experts, curated for a CX Manager lens.

---

## Format

Each item in the digest:

```
### N · Expert Name — Article/post title
**Who:** One sentence on who this person is — what they're known for.
**What:** What they said, with jargon explained in plain English in parentheses.
**Why it matters for you:** Framed specifically for someone managing a CX team.
**One question to sit with:** A reflection prompt tied to your work.
↗ [Read: title](url)
```

Ends with:
- **This week's one thing** — the single most important read if you only have 10 minutes
- **One reflection prompt** — a question that cuts across all items

## What makes a good item

**Include if:**
- Published in the past 14 days (stretch to 30 if a slow week)
- Directly relevant to managing a CX team (not just general business advice)
- Has a specific, actionable angle — not just "CX is important"
- Challenges a common assumption or surfaces new data

**Skip if:**
- Pure promotional content for a product or event
- Generic "top 10 tips" without original thinking
- Older content with no new angle

---

## Frequency

Weekly is the target. If a week is quiet (few new posts), it's fine to:
- Stretch to the past 2–3 weeks
- Go below 10 items (quality over volume)
- Surface an older piece that's newly relevant given something happening in CX
