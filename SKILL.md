## ANZ context note

Jaquie Scammell and Justin Tippett are both Australia-based. When their content appears in the digest, note any ANZ-specific context — Australian consumer expectations, local benchmarking data from ACXPA, or ANZ organisational culture. This is especially relevant for a CX Team Lead working in Melbourne.

## Selection criteria

**Include if:**
- Published in the past 14 days (stretch to 30 if a slow week)
- Directly relevant to managing a CX team
- Specific and actionable — not just "CX is important"
- Challenges a common assumption or surfaces new data

**Skip if:**
- Pure promotional content
- Generic listicles without original thinking
- Older content with no new angle

Max 10 items. Fewer sharp items beats 10 mediocre ones.

## Output format

```
### N · Expert Name — Article/post title
**Who:** One sentence on who this person is.
**What:** What they said. Jargon explained in plain English in parentheses.
**Why it matters for you:** Why a CX Team Lead managing a support team should care.
**One question to sit with:** A reflection prompt tied to real work.
↗ [Read: title](url)
```

End with:

```
## This week's one thing
[Single most important read — 2 sentences on why]

## Reflection prompt
[One question that cuts across all items this week]
```

## Notion destination

- **Parent:** CX Area Notes database
- **Title format:** `CX Weekly Digest — Week of [DD Mon YYYY]`
- **Type:** Research
- **Linked to:** CX area

## Tone

- Plain English, jargon explained inline
- Framed for a CX Team Lead — not analyst, not C-suite
- Direct and useful — no padding
